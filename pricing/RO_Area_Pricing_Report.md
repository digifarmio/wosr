## Methodology Notes

- **Area→county mapping**: From Corteva Lumiposa corn sales data (`2026 Lumiposa corn.xlsx`)
- **County pricing**: ERA5-Land 35yr (1990–2024) sigmoid ELF model, Marsh-calibrated (CF=0.3589)
- **Area LR**: Simple average of constituent county LRs (equal-weighted)
- **Hectare estimate**: bags × 10 kg/bag ÷ 3.5 kg/ha seeding rate
- **Premium**: LR × EUR 96 (sum insured) × 1.30 (commercial loading)
- **București**: Excluded (no Corteva sales area, no agricultural land)
