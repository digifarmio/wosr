## Methodology Notes

- **Area→district mapping**: From Corteva Slovakia sales data (Nils's GDrive spreadsheet)
- **District→kraj mapping**: Standard Slovak administrative division (72 okresy → 8 kraje)
- **Kraj pricing**: ERA5-Land 35yr (1990-2024) sigmoid ELF model, Marsh-calibrated (CF=0.3589)
- **Area LR**: Simple average of constituent kraje LRs (each kraj counted once regardless of district count)
- **Hectare estimate**: bags x 10 kg/bag / 3.5 kg/ha seeding rate
- **Premium**: LR x EUR 96 (sum insured) x 1.30 (commercial loading)
- **Note**: SK pricing CSV uses 8 kraje (self-governing regions), not 72 districts. Areas that span multiple kraje get the average of those kraje.
